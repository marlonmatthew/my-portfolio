About Me

My first exposure to web design was my high school zero period class where I began learning HTML and basic graphic design. Creating websites for personal endeavors was something I continued to enjoy doing throughout the years.

In 2016, I completed an internship with Linguistic Horizons where I learned how to utilize Wordpress and the many different plugins available. I took my first client in the local music community in 2017 and built a website with Wordpress and Elementor that helped him to attract more students and gain higher google search rankings.

I am completing a certification for a web development bootcamp in 2021 through the University of California, Riverside. I feel accomplished to have greatly expanded my knowledge and skills as a full stack web developer. My portfolio showcases many of the assignments and projects that I submitted throughout the bootcamp. Outside of the bootcamp, my portfolio also showcases freelance work I completed.

My other interests include beautiful nature hikes, hanging out at the beach, specialty blended cold brew beverages, amazing movies, delicious cuisine of all kinds, and most of all, music that evokes a multitude of different emotions and sensations.
